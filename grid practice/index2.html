Birds

The New Zealand rock wren is a small New Zealand wren endemic to the south island of New Zealand. Its Maori names include Piwauwau, matuitui and Tuke. Outside New Zealand it is sometimes known as the rockwren to distinguish it from the unrelated rock wren of North America.

Rock wrens are widely but patchily distributed through alpine and sub-alpine areas of the South Island. They are most common within the Southern Alps from Fiordland, through South Westland, and Mt Aspiring and Mt Cook National Parks
